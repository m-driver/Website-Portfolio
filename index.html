What is this webpage portfolio?

I am presently a student at the University of Tennessee - Knoxville anticipating to receive a Master of Science in Information Sciences in August of 2008. I began studying information sciences because of my long-time love of information organizations and a desire to build a career helping others.

While studying for my M.S.I.S. I would come across jobs that would request documented evidence of knowledge of programming and web skills like HTML, JavaScript, CSS, and others. I am building this website to demonstrate that knoweldge and ability.

Below is a series of "portfolio webpages" that were built to demonstrate knowledge of webpage construction, HTML, JavaScript, and CSS. Of course, one of the most wonderful thing about coding is that it is always changing and there are always new things to learn. I appreciate you taking the time to review my page and hope that my work demonstrates to you my ability to use these technologies.

This intro page

The first of the pages in my webpage portfolio is this one. It demonstrates the use of Flexbox coding for this list of webpages, the importation of fonts (this page uses @Font-Face to import Playfair Google font), creation of a sticky top menu, CSS coding for design elements, and a reactive design.

Cocoa and Marshmallow

This sample webpage is about my son's two guinea pig's: Cocoa and Marshmallow. This page also uses a responsive design and has a photo gallery with several pictures of the guinea pigs assigned by JavaScript, with both the picture URLs and the Alts entered through scripting. This page imports the Alex Brush font and uses floats and clearfix for the design rather than Flexbox.

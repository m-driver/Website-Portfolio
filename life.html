Conway's Game of Life

Conway's Game of Life is a simulation of life from mathmetitican John Conway where each cell in a grid is 'living' or 'dead'. Each generation any 'dead' cell with exactly three living neighbors becomes alive, and any 'live' cell without either one or two living neighbors dies. You can read more about it here.

So why a page about guinea pigs?

I can hear you asking that question and the truth is that this is a page about guinea pigs because I didn't know what else to make it about. So without further ado, allow me to introduce our two guinea pigs: Cocoa and Marshmallow!

Cocoa

This is Cocoa. He is Marshmallow's father and loves to eat the fluffy ends of the hay. He's smaller than his son, but is also much more outgoing and doesn't mind being picked up.

Marshmallow

This is Marshmallow. Marshmallow is quite a bit bigger than his father, but he is also much more skittish. He has gotten better about being picked up and petted, but still prefers to find a dark spot to hide in if given the choice.

The Story of Cocoa and Marshmallow

We got Cocoa and Marshmallow for Christmas 2018 as a surprise for our son. Cassandra has grown up with guinea pigs and so knew how to take care of them, but we were both surprised by how much cage standards had changed ( for the better) over the years. Cocoa and Marshmallow live in a spacious enclosure with lots of hay, a water botter, and several places to hide. They also get plenty of attention and are well loved.
